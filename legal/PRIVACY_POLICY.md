# Privacy Policy for GOAT Data Analyst

**Last Updated**: December 5, 2025

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Account creation date

### 1.2 Usage Data
- Files uploaded (name, size)
- Analysis history
- Usage statistics (number of analyses, storage used)

### 1.3 Automatically Collected
- IP address
- Browser type
- Device information
- Error logs

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the service
- Process your data analysis requests
- Send service-related notifications
- Improve our service
- Detect and prevent abuse

## 3. Data Storage

- Your data is stored securely on Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- Files are processed and deleted after analysis
- We do NOT permanently store your CSV data
- Account data is retained until account deletion

## 4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or share your personal data with third parties except:
- Supabase (database hosting)
- Stripe (payment processing)
- Sentry (error tracking)
- PostHog (analytics)

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Delete your account
- Export your data
- Opt-out of analytics

## 6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures:
- Encrypted passwords
- HTTPS connections
- Secure API authentication
- Regular security audits

## 7. Contact

For privacy concerns: privacy@goat-analyst.com
